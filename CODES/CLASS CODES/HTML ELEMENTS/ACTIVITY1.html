Fact 1: The amaizing octopus

Octopus have three hearts, nine brains, and blue blood. two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third heart pumps it to the rest of the body

How smart are Octopus?

Octopus can open jars, solve puzzels, and even recognise human faces. Scientists consider them one of the most intelligent invertebrates on the Earth.

Fact 2: Lightning strikes

Lightning strikes the earth about 100 times every singgle second. That is roughly 8 million strikes per day.

How hot is the lightning?

A single bolt of lightning is around 30,000 kelvin - five times hotter than the surface of the Sun.

Can lightning strikes twice?

Yes! Lightning absolutely can and does strike the same place more than once , espicially tall structures like towers and the trees.

Fact 3: The Deep Ocean.

More than 80% of the world's ocean remained un explored. Scientists believe there are millions of living species in the deep sea that we have never discovered.
